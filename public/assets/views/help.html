How do I subscribe to Bayelsa Public Square newsletter

To subscribe the Bayelsa Public Square newsletters, enter your email address into the newsletter subscribe box on the right of most pages on the Bayelsa Public Square and click the subscribe button.

How do I sign in into my account

To sign in, click on the sign in link at the top of each page or in the mobile menu.

How do I sign up for an account

To sign in, click on the sign up link at the top of each page or in the mobile menu.

How do I comment on articles

To comment on articles on Bayelsa Public Square, you must signed into you account.
Comments are found at the bottom of every article and can be view by click the comments button at the bottom of an article.

How do I report a comment on Bayelsa Public Square

When you see a comment that violates our policy, please use the “report” option located in the bottom-left corner of every comment. A moderator will then decide whether the comment stays on the site. Keep in mind that moderating can be subjective and final decisions rest with the site's administrators.

How do write or submit content to Bayelsa Public Square

You can write for the Bayelsa Public Square using our online post creator/editor, however you must have a Bayelsa Public Square account to do so. Posts/articles written and submitted using our post creator/editor will be reviewed and approved by the site's administrators and as such will not be immediately available to the public. Authors will be notified when their post/article is approved. Our online editor can be found here

How do I contact Bayelsa Public Square

You can contact the Bayelsa Public Square, by sending an email to bayelsapublicsquare@gmail.com
