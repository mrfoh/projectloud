About The Bayelsa Public Square

The Bayelsa Public Square (BPS) is an online platform for the public to share their opinions, views, and solutions to problems in Bayelsa State.

It allows indigenes, residents and concerned parties express their views and opinions on a wide array topics; goverment policy, projects, and general news and happenings in Bayelsa State.
